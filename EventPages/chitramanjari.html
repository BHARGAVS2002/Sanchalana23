Rules

  • 1. A team consisting of 3 members are to be registered.
  • 2.The event takes place with 4 rounds in it
  • 3.Round 1: 15 questions would be asked based on the Kannada Music Industry. In this the top 7 teams would be selected for Round 2.
  • 4.Round 2: In this round a word would be given by the Conveners and the teams have to sing songs starting from that word. ( the team which responds in less duration are awarded more points) and top 5 teams would be selected.
  • 5.Round 3: In this round the Charana of the song would be played and teams have to guess the Pallavi. Top 3 would be selected in this .
  • 6.Round 4: In this round 3 songs would be played in reverse loop and team has to guess the original song. The team with the highest points would be considered as winners
  • 7.The decisions made by the judges and Conveners are final.
