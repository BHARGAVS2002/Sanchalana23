Rules

  • 1. All teams must have at least 2 members and no more than 4 members.

  • 2.Teams must stay together during the Treasure Hunt and are encouraged to work together to solve the clues.

  • 3.Each team will be given “Clues” at the beginning of the treasure hunt. All of the treasure hunt clues will be in a paper. It will be up to the teams to decipher the clues and safely acquire the next clue from each of the locations. Each clue once solved will lead you to a location where you will be able to collect another “Treasure Hunt Clue”.

  • 4. Teams will be scored on the total value of the clues collected and the amount of time taken. Once all coins are collected teams must return to the finish in order to be counted as finished. Clue values will be determined based on the difficulty of the clue..

  • 5.Each team in the hunt is assigned a number. A paper with this number on it is provided to you at sign in on the day of the hunt. It will be used by Hunt Conveners to spot check compliance with the safety rules.

  • 6.“Clues” will be distributed to team members at that time and may be opened and the teams may start solving the clues and retrieving “Treasure Hunt Clues”

  • 7.Disqualification will result from any of the following
    a. Damaging any property b. Interfering with other teams or their members in any way c. Copying from other teams. d. Stealing from other teams .
