Description

A luxury plane has taken off from Bangalore at 1100 hours. It is bound to reach Shimla at 1400 hours. It is filled with famous personalities, people we talk about every day. Cricketers, Actors, Musicians, Stock Market Giants and influential personalities. Alas! The plane has a bird strike and has lost the use of both its engines. There is only ONE parachute on board. Meaning, only ONE celebrity can be saved. But, who do we save?

Rules

  • Round 1:
    Preparations Rules:
    1) Participants will be assigned a famous personality on the spot and will be given two minutes to prepare about the personality.
    2) Phones and the Internet can be used to prepare.
    3) Participants are not allowed to leave the venue after receiving their personality.
    4) Participants are not allowed to discuss their personalities with anyone.

    Presentation Rules:
    1) 8 celebrities will line up and convince the audience and other participants that they should be allowed to survive.
    2) Participants will be given 90 seconds for this presentation.
    3) After 90 seconds, the judges can question the participant.
    4) No vulgarity is allowed. If used, it will lead to direct disqualification.
    5) Winners of each batch of 8 participants will qualify for the FINALS.
    6) The deciding factors will be the Content and Quality of Speech, Voice Modulation, Presentation Skills, and the ability to sway the crowd.

    Round 2:
    This would be conducted on the results of the first round and the winners are awarded accordingly.
