Rules

  • 1. Melody round (Indian vocal)
  • 2.Duet round (Indian Vocal)
  • 3.Performance round (Indian vocal or Western Vocal)

  • Indian vocal:

  • 1.The participant shall sing any song of his/ her choice to showcase their talent.
  • 2.Karaoke/CD can be played.
  • 3.If anyone is interested in playing instruments of their choice or willing to make anyone play on their behalf, they are free to do so. Note that no extra points will be awarded for this.
  • 4.Total time duration is 3+2.
  • 5.Jury’s decision is final.

  • Western Vocal:

  • 1.Maximum number of accompanists is two.
  • 2.Language of the song to be selected is only English.
  • 3.Duration is 3+2 minutes.
  • 4.4 minutes for instruments or stage setting.
  • 5.Jury’s decision is final.

  • Note: Prelims for the event will be done as per rules of solo singing.
