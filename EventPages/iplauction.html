Rules and Regulations:

  • A group event.

  • Maximum of 3 participants can be in a team

  • The event takes place in 2 Rounds.

    • Round 1 (Crick Quiz)

  • 1. There would be a general quiz round of 30 questions related to IPL would be pitched.

  • 2. Top 10 teams of the best score would be qualified for the next round.

    • Round 2 (Auction Round)

  • 1. In this round the participants have to name their teams (original IPL team names are not entertained)

  • 2. The teams would be given a virtual money

  • 3. Participants have to bid for 13 players (consisting of 4 batsmen + 3 bowlers + 3 all rounders + 1 wicket keeper)

  • 4. Conveners decision stand final.

  • 5. Remaining rules are explained on the event day.
