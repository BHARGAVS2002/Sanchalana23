FAQ

How do I register for Sanchalana'23 ?

Registration can be done through the official Sanchalana 2k23 website. By filling in their personal details, participant can register for any event by clicking on that particular event and following google form link.

What should I do after completing registration?


After registering, you will get an email to verify your registration. Keep following our updates on social media and email.

Is the entry free for all ? Who can attend ?


Entry to Sanchalana will be absolutely free (Only fee has to be paid for events). Only college students (Degree and above students) are allowed

Can I cancel my event registration?

No, once registration cannot be cancelled.

How payment can be made for event registration ?


Payment can be made in both online and offline method. For any queries related to payment contact event coordinators.
